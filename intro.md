Hello Musicologie!

My name is Zach Ross, and I'm a drummer living out of Alpharetta, Georgia. I've been playing drumset and classical percussion for over 20 years and have been teaching privately in various subject matters over the past 8 years. Reading through the Musicologie website, I found that your philosophy and methods deeply aligned with the way I view teaching. Especially with regards to creating an immersive atmosphere where people feel safe to grow from their own mistakes, I think we all work best when we can pursue joy free from judgement. That, and the best motivator comes from curiosity. It's what made the company stand out to me the most as I looked through the internet for postings.

Over the past couple years, I've found teaching (and learning) to be a great passion of mine. Just watching the expression as a new drummer is able to play their first version of boots and cats or hearing them talk about their first jam session...it's a feeling like no other. I'd started my own company in 2025 to explore this avenue more, mainly focusing on providing music education to beginner adults and coaching to experienced professionals. While this has been fruitful, I've been eager to bring this passion to a broader community and focus my energy towards becoming a better teacher. 

Drumming has been a core part of my life ever since I started playing my first snare at 8. Whether it was marching band, wind ensemble, jazz band, or a rock band playing at a dive bar, I loved it. It was there for me through every graduation, breakup, and teenage temper tantrum, and so I remained disciplined to it even through by brief tenure as a computer scientist. I love finding new ways to approach my own instrument and bringing this same curiosity to developing other's potential. Even when I sit at the kit and practice paradiddles for the ten-thousandth time, I still feel like I learn something new. Outside of music, I spend alot of time in different disciplines. I love meditation, camping, running (working towards my first marathon!), spending time with my dog, and watching re-runs of *Seinfeld*. I've also recently become deeply invested in Jazz culture and have been spending alot of time attending jam sessions in the Atlanta area.

Thank you for taking the time to read this. Hope to hear from you soon.

Sincerely,
Zach Ross
