## Summary

<!-- Summarize the month in 1000 characters or less. -->
This month was a slight derailing from intenions. Growing more into the social circles I'd so long desired began briniging up new insecurities which I'd never fully dealt with before (identifying with being bullied and needing someone to own me), but also greater information on the optimal direction. The obsession and possession parts of my fantasies became highlighted further and further until I was pushed into relapse. But then I also went to Caleb's bonfire and made many new friends, connected with a group at the run club, and all around have grown much more confident around people. The recurring conflict was a question of what does discipline look like in this new world? I've been more motivated than ever thanks to the great people around me (and lack of escape) but I think having this new support to lean on made it easy to act mindlessly. Most of the month can be summarized by this one quote: "You know it's always about not being able to tolerate the real world and so I had this place to retreat where I was safe and I was wanted and I was necessary and I had attention. But now it's getting to a point where I'm strong enough to withstand life and so it feels like there's no purpose to holding onto it because I don't need it as a retreat or a safety or security anymore."
