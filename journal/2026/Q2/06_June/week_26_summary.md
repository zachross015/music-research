# Week 26

## Summary

<!-- Summarize the week in 1000 characters or less. -->

Spent this week embodying the principles I'd learned in The Courage to Be Disliked and The Inner Game of Tennis. Both offered a meaningful look into the inner game, very closely connected to stoicism. In this lens, it became clear to me the ways I was excusing certain behaviors to keep me socially distant. Like manufacturing fear in conversations with my family. When I began letting Self 2 take the wheel more often, I found myself achieving incredible things that I'd never even thought to do, and improvements where I couldn't explain what was better. This was both a powerful and painful thing, as I realized the power visualization has held on me and how, even goals I WANTED could turn out harmful. Eg desire to play drums all day could be achieved by manufacturing obsession, the desire to wake up early can be achieved but if enough sleep isn't baked into the visual then the rest of the day is ineffective. But on the other side, I tested the theory on my hill climbs, and was shown the absolute power of focus. I didn't exactly hit the goal I set, but I went farther than ever before. Ended the week in a gauntlet of work -> Nomad rehearsal -> work, which ended up being great. In the light of it all, I've felt more connected to community than ever before and have begun seeing the powers that generosity, flow, and community have in the world.
