## Summary

<!-- Summarize the week in 1000 characters or less. -->

It was a long and socially exhausting week. I started the week on a high note, focusing exclusively on my chores so that I could go into the rest of the week feeling prepared and refreshed. Something about this effort and reflecting on my half marathon made me realize the deeper nature of achieving goals: they require deep commitment even when the plan is wrong. I'd been going about goals by making a plan and not adhering to it. Seeing that now, I realize that the problem was in picking unclear goals (eg earn $200 from gigs) with ambiguous execution strategies, while also altering my goals midway through when different details made themselves known. Because of this, I began structuring my goals and achievement strategies to reflect this. For example, my goal for music these next two months was changed from earn $200 to getting one new client through daily social media connection, with the strategy being to post, comment, share, or message people 10 times a day (ie 10 actions). With a definite plan, if I fail then I can revise the system, but I need to fail to learn. Outside of this, the week was loaded with Joey's graduation plans. Wednesday with grandpa, Thursday over at Kennesaw, and again Friday night at Kennesaw, then yesterday at PorchFest. There was an increasing decline in my mental state over the course of the week as I continued pushing my own social growth through a vulnerable insta reel, selling one coworker on the idea of swapping shifts and staying present in the "competition" (thank you Kaylen), the SM reps, showing up for the run club, staying present in conversation with Grandpa, andn attending PorchFest. The good news is that my ability to stay present in all conversations is growing, but the weakness being revealed is the same in one-on-one conversations, jazz improv, and my ability to go with the flow in social settings. The most unfortunate part of this week was how little practice I got. I found many ways to practice outside of playing the kit, but it upsets me looking back on the week with only 2-3 dilligent practices. Hoping to get more in this week.
