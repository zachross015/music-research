## Summary

<!-- Summarize the month in 1000 characters or less. -->
The primary theme of this month has been shifting from an outcome-oriented mindset to one of deeper presence. I began the month in recovery from the half-marathon, a goal which left me with the wisdom of how deep of dedication is required to achieve a goal. Noting this, I revised what worked/failed over the past couple years (wrt my music career) and began planning a new method for gaining new clients: by focusing creating a goal and detailing a plan of controllable actions to execute (in this case, gain 1 client by having 10 SM actions every day). The return to SM revealed a plethora of insecurities I'd never wrestled with: Jess potentially having a new BF, better players than me on insta, and asking for help around my weaknesses. Ultimately, I used these as opportunities to practice healthier coping via meditation and running.

The consequence of switching to a more present mindset (with a deeper Stoic study) is that I'd realized I'd been holding back other avenues of my life to force the outcome I desired (career musician). I'd realized this was creating alot of unnecessary friction and assumed that if I were to advance elsewhere (relationship, promotion, etc) that I would not enjoy music enough to continue, or at the very least not enough to grow. The other side is that it's possible to advance these in a way that also accelerates progress, but only by dealing with what is happening right now, not what might happen later. So I applied for a promotion, started making a resume for music education, and found a selection of masters to learn from. 

Proceeding to the end of the month, I continued stretching my social growth further and further. I "invited" (announced) a gig to ten friends and family. I used what resources I had to give a shift to Kaylen (thanks for taking it), showed up for run club and PorchFest, and bailed on friends when I was socially exhausted. I even posted multiple vulnerable reels, to great reception! And I'm entering the next month feeling the strength of presence and a new security in social media. That, and a new perspective on developing stronger intuition.
