## Summary

<!-- Summarize the week in 1000 characters or less. -->

This was the first week following the half marathon. I spent the week deciding a new focus and planning the route there, which ended up being a straightforward process. I reflected on the things that worked leading up to this point, which invariably pointed out social media's value that I've disregarded. Simultanteously, Peak showed my need for a great coach and a deeper delve into stoicism pointed out the overthinking and analysis paralysis that's kept me from bigger and better. Going over these, I formulated a plan for getting more gigs, growing more with the community, and upgrading my station in life. The combination of this and my first venture back into social media brought out a new wave of unique insecurities. Watching Colton play so well and being younger than me opened up the pain well around my playing. Then, seeing Jess with a (potentially) new BF opened wounds I'd forgotten about. Both led me to grief and insecurity. Many parallels exist between the insecurity I feel around other players and that of this breakup. Rather than numbing it, I sat with these pains this time. Much of the weekend was dedicated to recovery, espeically questioning my methods of recovery and recognizing how powerful friendship is in this endeavor. I spent the last couple days recounting the entirety of the relaionship, warts and all, in a way that revealed the guilt I suffered. In spite of this, I ended in good spirits. I recovered. I spent more time in curiosity, listening to Bea Porges for the first time. Such beautiful music.
